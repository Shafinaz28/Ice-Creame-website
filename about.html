About Us

We are passionate about creating delightful ice creams crafted with love and quality ingredients. Our mission is to bring happiness in every scoop to our customers.

"Real ingredients make real ice cream"

Our Heritage

What began in 1985 as Grandma Maria's homemade ice cream recipe for her grandchildren has blossomed into a beloved local institution. Using only the finest ingredients from trusted local farmers, we've stayed true to our roots while innovating new flavors that excite the palate.

Today, our small-batch ice cream is still churned the old-fashioned way, with care and attention to every scoop. We refuse to compromise on quality, which is why you'll never find artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives in our products.

Our Promise

  • 100% natural ingredients from ethical sources
  • Small-batch production for premium quality
  • Sustainable practices that protect our planet
  • A portion of profits supports local food banks

Fun Fact

We use over 2,000 vanilla beans each week! Each bean is hand-selected from sustainable farms in Madagascar.

Our Process

Every scoop tells a story of craftsmanship and care

Ingredient Selection

We personally visit farms to select only the freshest, highest quality ingredients. Our dairy comes from grass-fed cows within 50 miles of our creamery, and fruits are picked at peak ripeness.

Small-Batch Preparation

Each batch is carefully crafted in our copper kettles, never exceeding 10 gallons to ensure perfect consistency and flavor development. Our slow-churn method preserves the natural textures.

Flavor Development
