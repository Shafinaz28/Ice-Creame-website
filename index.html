Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies depending on the product type, but typically starts at 100 units. For custom formulations or private label orders, we can discuss minimums based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer private labeling or custom branding?

Yes! We provide private labeling and full custom branding options tailored to your needs. This includes custom packaging design, logo placement, and even custom flavor development for your brand.

How do you ensure cold-chain delivery?

We use insulated packaging and temperature-controlled vehicles to maintain cold-chain integrity. Our logistics partners are specially trained in handling frozen products, and we monitor temperatures throughout the delivery process.

What are your packaging sizes?

We offer a range of packaging options from 250g pouches to 5kg bulk packs depending on the product. Our most popular sizes for businesses are 1L and 5L tubs, which are easy to store and serve from.

Are your products suitable for vegetarians?

Yes, all our ice creams are vegetarian-friendly. We also offer a range of vegan options made with plant-based milks and natural ingredients.

How long can I store your ice cream?

Our ice creams have a shelf life of 12 months when stored at -18°C or below. Once opened, we recommend consuming within 2 weeks for best quality.
